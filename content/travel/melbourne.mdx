# Melbourne

Melbourne is Australia's food and culture capital — a city that takes its coffee seriously, its street art personally, and its laneways religiously. If Sydney is the beaches, Melbourne is the restaurants. Come hungry.

## Tips

- **Weather is unpredictable.** Melbourne famously has "four seasons in one day." Layer up and carry a light jacket even in summer.
- **Laneways are the point.** Melbourne's best cafes, bars, and restaurants hide in narrow alleys. Explore Hosier Lane, Degraves Street, and Centre Place.
- **Tram rides are free in the CBD.** The Free Tram Zone covers the central city — just hop on and off.
- **Coffee culture is serious.** Don't order a Starbucks. Find a local roaster.

## Food & Drink

- ★ **Soi 38** — Authentic Thai street food. Spicy noodles and bold curries done right.
- ★ **Homm** — Thai bingsu and shokupan (Japanese milk bread).
- ★ **Sebastian's Kokiguri** — Korean shaved ice desserts.
- ★ **Karriton Dessert** — Filipino-inspired sweets. Get the ube ice cream on the sweet bread.
- ★ **Bakemono Bakery** — Japanese bakery on Little Lonsdale Street. Flaky croissants, soft breads, and unique donuts that transport you to Tokyo.
- ★ **Higher Grounds** — Excellent brunch spot.
- **Dragon Hotpot** — Great for a hearty, customizable hotpot session with friends.
- **Kumo** — Soufflé pancakes.
- **Two Birds One Stone (South Yarra)** — Trendy dining.
- **Buza Chicken** — Fried chicken spot.
- **Marios** — Known for fried gnocchi.
- **Bar Holiday** — Cocktails and vibes.
- **Hareruya** — Japanese.
- **Wong's Late Night Hot Pot** — Late-night hotpot spot.
- **Nana Thai** — Thai.
- **Fuumi Fuumi** — Known for the beef stew.
- **Bun Bun Bakery** — Pastries and baked goods.
- **Publique Bakery** — Artisan bakery.

## Things to Do

- ★ **ACMI (Australian Centre for the Moving Image)** — Melbourne's hub for film, TV, and digital culture. Interactive exhibits and immersive screenings.
- **National Gallery of Victoria (NGV)** — Australia's oldest and largest art museum. Both classical and contemporary art.
- **Swanson Street** — Main shopping thoroughfare. High-end retail, dining, and landmarks.
- **Yarra River** — Walk, bike, or cruise along the riverbank for great city views.
- **Thai Town** — Explore Melbourne's Thai food hub.

## Day Trips

- **Great Ocean Road** — One of the world's most scenic coastal drives. See the Twelve Apostles, Loch Ard Gorge, and dramatic cliffside views. Allow a full day.
- **Mornington Peninsula** — Wine region, hot springs, and coastal walks about an hour from the city.
