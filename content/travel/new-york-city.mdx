# New York City

NYC doesn't need a sales pitch. This guide is organized by neighborhood and focused on what matters most: where to eat. The city moves fast, the food is incredible, and every borough has something worth crossing town for. Bring your appetite and comfortable shoes.

## Tips

- **Dorsia App** — Use it for snagging reservations at exclusive restaurants.
- **Walk everywhere.** The subway is great for distance, but neighborhoods reveal themselves on foot.
- **Cash in Flushing.** Most of Flushing's best spots are cash-only.

## Midtown

- **Penn Station / Madison Square Garden** — Transit hub and entertainment venue.
- **Madison Square Park** — Nice green space, home of the original Shake Shack.
- **Bryant Park** — Midtown oasis. Great for a break between meals.
- **MoMA** — The Museum of Modern Art. World-class collection.
- **The Met** — Massive. You could spend days here.
- **Museum of Natural History** — Iconic. Great for families.

## Flushing, Queens

Flushing is one of NYC's greatest food destinations — an authentic slice of China right in Queens. Most spots are cash-only, so hit the ATM first.

### Must-Try Street Food

- **1 Golden Pancake** — Corn croissant and egg tarts.
- **Fu Yuan** — Curry fish balls and cheung fun.
- **Xie Bao** — Crab roe over rice or noodles.
- **Pan Bao 66** — Shen jian bao (pan-fried soup dumplings).
- **Liu Liu Sheng Jian Wang** — More shen jian bao, different style.
- **Eight Jane** — Jian bing (Chinese crepes).
- **Gao Xiao Pa** — Rou jia mo (Chinese meat sandwiches).
- **Ming Wei** — Jian bing guo zi.

### Bakeries & Drinks

- **Taning Lemon Tea** — Refreshing lemon tea.
- **Mr Tu's Bakery** — Pastries.
- **Fay Da Bakery** — Get the taro puff.
- **Lee & Bai Chinese Buns Shop** — Chengdu braised pork buns.

- **Taipei Hong** — Gua bao (Taiwanese pork buns).
- **Corner 28** — Char siu over rice.
- **King's Kitchen** — Claypot rice.
- **He Nan Feng Wei** — Chinese regional cuisine.
- **Siam Thai** — Thai.
- **Tai Er Sichuan Cuisine** — Spicy Sichuan.
- **Alley 41** — Taiwanese.
- **Mikiya Wagyu Shabu House** — Japanese hotpot.
- **Xu Chu** — Chinese.
- **Mimisa** — Chinese.
- **Haidilao Hotpot** — Chain hotpot (but good).
- **Fish With You** — Seafood.
- **Sing Choi Kee** — Chinese.
