# Vietnam

Vietnam is one of those places that hits every sense at once — the hum of motorbikes, the smell of pho at 7am, lanterns reflecting off water at night. This guide covers three regions: Hoi An (the charming old town), Da Nang (beaches and mountains), and Saigon (the chaotic, food-obsessed southern capital). Each has its own rhythm.

## Hoi An

The jewel of central Vietnam. A small town with lantern-lit streets, centuries-old architecture, and some of the best tailoring in the world.

### Things to Do

- ★ **Hoi An Memories** — A theatrical show bringing Hoi An's history to life through music and dance.
- ★ **Ba Na Hills** — A full day trip with carnival rides, an alpine ride in Fantasy Land, Popmart Castle, the iconic Golden Bridge (held up by giant stone hands), a towering Buddha statue, and the 4 Seasons Buffet.
- **Explore Old Town** — Wander the lantern-lit alleys, historic architecture, and vibrant shops.
- **Japanese Bridge** — An iconic architectural landmark blending Japanese and local design.
- **Lantern Market** — Visit at night for colorful lanterns and handcrafted souvenirs.
- **Lantern Boats at Night** — Magical. Float on the river in a lantern-lit boat under the night sky.
- **45 Thu Tailoring** — Get custom-tailored clothing with a wide fabric selection.
- **Jimmy's Jewelry** — Craft a custom ring as a memento.

### Food & Drink

- **Com Linh** — Vietnamese twist on tapas. Small plates, big flavors.
- **Hygge Coffee** — Cozy café inspired by Danish hygge.
- **Old River Restaurant Bar** — Riverside views with Vietnamese and international food.
- **Miss Ly Cafe** — Traditional Hoi An food in a homey atmosphere.
- **Dudu Cafe** — Chic and modern, good for unwinding.

## Da Nang & Surroundings

The coastal city between Hoi An and the mountains.

### Things to Do

- **Monkey Mountain** — Home to Linh Ung Pagoda and the towering Lady Buddha statue overlooking the ocean.
- **Marble Mountain** — Caves and temples with a journey that takes you "from hell to heaven."
- **Da Nang Cathedral** — A charming pink cathedral with French colonial influences.
- **Dragon Bridge Performance** — The bridge breathes fire and water at night. A must-see spectacle.

### Food & Drink

- **Moc Quan Seafood Restaurant** — Fresh seafood with local flavors in a laid-back setting.
- **Son Tra Night Market** — Street food, local goods, and live performances.
- **GOLO Coffee & AP** — Contemporary café with a relaxed vibe.
- **Dng.coffee** — Minimalist specialty coffee spot.

### Relax

- **Serene Spa & Massage (Hoi An Branch)** — Traditional Vietnamese massage.
- **Luxury Healthy Spa & Massage** — Wellness-focused treatments.

## Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City)

The southern capital. Chaotic, fast, loud — and absolutely packed with incredible food.

### Things to Do

- **Independence Palace** — Historical landmark with insight into Vietnam's political history.
- **Saigon Central Post Office** — Classic French colonial architecture.
- **Book Street** — A charming pedestrian street lined with bookstores and cafés.
- **Cu Chi Tunnels Tour** — Explore the underground tunnel network from the Vietnam War.
- **Cafe Apartments** — A former apartment building turned into a multi-story collection of cafes and shops.
- **New Playground** — Hip indoor market with trendy local brands.

### Food & Drink

- **Pho Vietnam** — Classic pho. Authentic flavors.
- **Pho Bom** — Rich pho with quality ingredients.
- ★ **Banh Mi Hyun Hoa** — Some of the best banh mi in Saigon. Crispy bread, incredible fillings.
- ★ **Bep Me In (Michelin Guide)** — Authentic Vietnamese in a warm, rustic setting.
- **Quan Ngon Bun Cha** — Hanoi-style bun cha with grilled pork, herbs, and vermicelli.
- **O Tinh House** — Home-cooked Vietnamese comfort food.
- **The Gangs Central** — Fusion Asian flavors in a lively setting.
- **Sai Gon Kitchen Vina** — Quality local cuisine.
- **Mon Moi** — Comforting hotpot and spareribs.
- **Apartment Shops (Bleu Monkey Cafe)** — Quirky cafes inside a converted apartment building.

### Coffee

Saigon's café scene is world-class.

- **The Workshop Coffee** — Top-notch brews in a bright, creative space.
- **Cafe Linh** — Traditional yet stylish.
- **% Arabica** — Iconic Japanese coffee chain.
- **Goodday Coffee** — Cozy with a creative interior.
- **AM.PM Cafe** — Stylish drinks, any time of day.
- **Cafe Mon Ami** — French-inspired with great pastries.
- **Little Hanoi Egg Coffee** — The famous egg coffee — creamy and rich.
- **96b Concept Cafe** — Art meets coffee culture.
- **Cong Cafe** — Retro chain inspired by Vietnamese history.
- **La.Zero Saigon** — Café and art gallery.
- **Beanthere Cafe** — Quiet and aromatic, great for working.

### Relax

- **Hum Spa** — Therapeutic massages and wellness treatments.
