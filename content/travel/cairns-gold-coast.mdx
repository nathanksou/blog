# Cairns & Gold Coast

Two very different vibes on the tropical end of Australia. Cairns is the gateway to the Great Barrier Reef and the Daintree Rainforest — rugged, natural, and adventure-focused. The Gold Coast is surf culture, theme parks, and nightlife. Both are worth your time.

## Cairns

### Tips

- **Motion sickness pills.** The boat to the Great Barrier Reef takes about 2 hours each way, and it gets rough. Bring two pills — one for the way out, one for the return. Tours sell them onboard for $8–10 AUD, but bring your own.
- **Get dive-certified before you go.** If you try to get certified on the boat and fail, some operators won't let you switch to snorkeling. This happened on our trip and it's common.
- **The reef is alive.** Don't believe the doom and gloom entirely. Online photos exaggerate the color vibrancy — colors appear darker unless the light hits perfectly — but the marine life is incredible. You might even spot a shark.

### Things to Do

- ★ **Great Barrier Reef (Snorkeling or Diving)** — The main event. Book a full-day tour. Respect the reef.
- **Cairns Esplanade Lagoon** — A massive public swimming pool with ocean views. Great for lounging in the main tourist area.
- **Daintree Rainforest** — Didn't make it personally, but it's highly recommended by everyone.

### Food & Drink

- ★ **Prawn Stars** — A floating seafood restaurant on the wharf. Book ahead if you can. Massive seafood platters.
- **Salt House** — Small plates and shareables are the move here. The mac and cheese, kingfish sashimi, and tiger prawns stood out. Skip the mains.

## Gold Coast

A top tropical destination — think surfing, beach vibes, nightlife, and theme parks. If you've been to Hawaii or Cancún, the Gold Coast hits similar notes but with an Australian twist.

### Things to Do

- **Surfer's Paradise** — The heart of Gold Coast beach culture. Surfing, dining, shopping, nightlife.
- **Broadbeach** — Trendy area with a more relaxed vibe. Great cafes and boutiques.
- **Movieworld** — Movie-themed rides, shows, and attractions.
- **Dreamworld** — Thrill rides, animal encounters, and family-friendly attractions.

### Food & Drink

- ★ **Nicholini's Italian** — Solid Italian in a cozy setting.
- **Rumi's** — Mediterranean flavors, vibrant dishes.
- **Hakata Ramen** — Authentic ramen with flavorful broths. Great after a day at the beach.
